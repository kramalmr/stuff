TK, SD Islam Al-Falaah

2012 - 2021

I spent my early years at TK and SD Islam Al-Falaah in Ciputat. I have never been curious to any specific field of study but I have always been interested in learning new things. Since then, I have been also playing games and watching foreign cartoons which led me to having a good understanding in English and it also gave me a wide interests in playing more games. At the last year of my Elementary School, Ive opened a new interest in wanting to know how games codes, commands, etc. work which then i gave huge interests on. And now i knew that it was a good choice for me to learn more about it.

Glancing back to my years of Elementary School, i remember that i was always curious to know how games are made and how they work which led me to learning a bit of it, but i never really focused on learning it and it was futile work to me at the time, because i had no idea where to head next.

SMP, SMA Pondok Tahfizh Plus Abu Dzar

2021 - now

As I continue my educational journey, I am excited to pursue my interests in programming and graphic design. Although here that the school also provides me with religious lessons, they also gave a focus on programming and coding. This is the reason why i decided to join this school.

My time at this school made me more independent for myself and non-relying on others. I was able to learn more about myself and my surroundings and what purpose i want and i was destined to achieve. I am still countinuing to be more capable to achieve my goals and dreams.

At SMP, SMA Pondok Tahfizh Plus Abu Dzar, I have been learning more about programming and coding. The school provides me with a good environment to learn and focus on my interests. I am excited to continue learning and growing in this field. I am excited to see where this journey will take me and what kind of projects I will be able to create in the future.
